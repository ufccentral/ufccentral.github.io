Demetrious "Mighty Mouse" Johnson (25-4-1)

Demetrious Johnson is a former UFC Flyweight Champion and ONE Flyweight Champion. He started wrestling at a young age and eventually began training MMA as a hobby. He made his MMA debut in 2006. He later signed with WEC in 2010, which merged with the UFC. Johnson made his UFC debut in 2011. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest fighters of all time and the greatest flyweight of all time.

  • Most title defenses (11)
  • Most consecutive wins (13)
  • Most submissions in title fights
