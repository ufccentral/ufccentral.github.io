Islam Makhachev (28-1)

Islam Makhachev is the current welterweight champion and former lightweight champion of the UFC. He has been competing in professional mixed martial arts since 2010. He became a combat sambo world champion in 2016, as well as a two-time Russian national champion in 2014 and 2016.

Lightweight Records

  • Longest winning streak (14)
  • Most title-fight victories (5)
  • Most successful title defenses (4)
  • Highest significant-strike accuracy (59.5%)
  • Tied longest winning streak in UFC history (16)

Notable Wins

  • Charles Oliveira via submission with an arm-triangle choke, winning the Lightweight Belt.
  • Alexander Volkanovski (x2) - once by unanimous decision and another via knockout.
  • Dustin Porier via submission with a brabo choke.
  • Jack Della Maddalena via unanimous decision, winning the Welterweight Belt.
