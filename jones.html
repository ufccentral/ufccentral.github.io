Jon "Bones" Jones (28-1-1)

Jon Jones is a retired champion of both the Light Heavyweight and Heavyweight divisions. He became the youngest champion ever at 23 years old, winning the light heavyweight belt over Mauricio Rua. He became the eighth fighter to hold titles in two weight classes. He is also the only fighter to beat 5 former champions consecutively. Most regard Jones as the greatest mixed martial artist of all time.
