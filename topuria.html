Ilia "El Matador" Topuria (17-0)

Ilia is the current Lightweight and former Featherweight Champion. Born in Halle Westfalen, Germany, Topuria began wrestling at the age of seven. He eventually began to train Brazilian jiu-jitsu at 15, when he soon switched to MMA.

Lightweight Records

  • Longest winning streak (14)
  • Most title-fight victories (5)
  • Most successful title defenses (4)
  • Highest significant-strike accuracy (59.5%)
  • Tied longest winning streak in UFC history (16)

Notable Wins

  • Charles Oliveira by knockout, winning the Lightweight Belt.
  • Max Holloway by knockout.
  • Alexander Volkanovski by knockout, winning the featherweight belt.
  • Ryan Hall by knockout.
